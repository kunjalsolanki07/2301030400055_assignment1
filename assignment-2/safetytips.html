Candle Water Experiment

Demonstrating how water can be drawn up a candle when heated.

  • Use a candle, shallow dish, and glass jar.
  • Light the candle and cover with a jar.
  • Watch as water rises into the jar.

Bridge Strength Project

Test how much weight a simple bridge can hold.

  • Use popsicle sticks and glue to build a bridge.
  • Test its strength by adding weight.

Water Cycle Project

Demonstrating how the water cycle works.

  • Use a clear cup, warm water, and plastic wrap.
  • Watch as water evaporates and condenses.

Solar System Model

Create a model of the solar system with planets.

  • Use styrofoam balls and paint.
  • Arrange planets with string in order.

Blooming Flower Craft

Create a paper flower that "blooms" with water.

  • Cut paper petals and fold them.
  • Place in water and watch it bloom!

Safety Tips

  • Never leave candles unattended.
  • Use non-toxic glue and paint.
  • Handle scissors carefully.
  • Be cautious with warm or hot water.
  • Use safe glue that is child-friendly.
